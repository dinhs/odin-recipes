Ramen Recipe

Description

Ramen is a simple and fast meal that can be prepared with little effort. Are you in need of something fast and filling that leaves your stomach feeling warm? Instant ramen sounds like the answer that you need.

Ingredients

  • Cup of instant ramen
  • Water
  • Garnishings (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a sauce pan.
  2. Lift the lid of the ramen and fill the container to the indicated line with the water.
  3. Let the container sit for 2 minutes.
  4. Add any garnishings of your choosing and enjoy!
