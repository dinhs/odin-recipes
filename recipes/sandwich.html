Sandwich Recipe

Description

The sandwich is a mainstay staple of the American lunch. It's portable, easy to store, and simple to make. We'll be going over how to make a relatively plain sandwich that is perfect for a picnic out.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Meat of your choice
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Condiments of your choice

Steps

  1. Place 2 pieces of bread next to each other.
  2. Place the meat of your choice on one piece of bread.
  3. Put cheese on top of the meat.
  4. Follow the cheese with a couple slices of tomato.
  5. Apply the condiments of your choice to the other piece of bread.
  6. Your sandwich is ready to be eaten!
