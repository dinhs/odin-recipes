Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian dish that serves the masses of Italy and America with its delicious flavor. Today we'll be teaching you how to boil noodles and open a jar of Prego sauce. Strap in and put on your favorite apron.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of thin spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Water
  • 1 12 oz Prego sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the water. Salt to taste.
  2. Add the thin spaghetti noodles. Cook for 10 minutes for perfect al dente consistency. Make sure to stir every couple of minutes.
  3. Heat the prego sauce in a small sauce pan on low heat.
  4. Once the noodles are done, drain the water and leave the noodles in the pot.
  5. Add the sauce and mix it with the noodles. It's ready to serve!
